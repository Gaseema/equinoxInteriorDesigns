ABOUT US

Equinox Interior Designs is an interior design firm founded by Gaseema Ndung'u and is known for its unconstrained approach in actualizing design concepts while respecting conventional and traditional design philosophies. Our strength lies in the ability to conceptualizedistinctive environments that enhances the vision and branding clients aspire , creating environment that would touch the subconscious of the consumer and occupant.Since the firm began its career in interior design, it has broadened its practice to Creative Direction, Architectural Design Direction,and Project Development Consulting. While the firm is based in Nairobi, projects have taken them to Uganda, South Afreica, Nigeria and other parts of Africa.

The idea behind our concept was to offer you an extensive range of beautiful products under one roof and in a real boutique feel. First and foremost, Equinox Design Agency is about people, but the product takes a lot of room in our philosophy, from the design of the object itself to the variety of our lines, we make sure to carefully attend every step of its way, from the discovery to the installation.
